The Thunder God

Diving into the Mythology of Thor



Thor's the son of Odin and Jord. He is the strongest of the Aesir.

He is the thunder god. His hall is Bilskirnir. Thor is often portrayed as

a very strong and hearty man, with a long flowing red beard and

peircing eyes. In Norse Mythology, he is locked in a constant struggle

against Jormungand, a senister serpant.
